1. Letter of Demand Template Configuration

You must be able to configure the actual legal document.

Required fields:

Template name
Language (English / Afrikaans)
Letter content
Municipality details
Attorney details
Payment instructions
Payment deadline (days)

Variables normally required:

Debtor name
Account number
Property address
Outstanding balance
Interest
Due date
Municipality contact details

Without a configurable template you cannot legally maintain different demand letters.

2. Legal Timing Configuration

The system must control when notices are issued.

Typical settings:

Days overdue before first demand
Days overdue before final demand
Days before legal handover
Days before summons

Example configuration:

First reminder: 14 days
Final demand: 30 days
Legal handover: 60 days

3. Communication Channels

Legal notices must record how they were delivered.

Configuration required:

Send SMS
Send Email
Send WhatsApp
Generate PDF letter
Print letter

Each must be configurable per notice stage.

Example:

Final demand
SMS: Yes
Email: Yes
PDF: Yes

4. Proof of Delivery Settings

Courts often require proof of notice delivery.

System must log:

Communication date
Communication channel
Recipient details
Delivery status

Without this, legal actions can be dismissed.

5. Interest and Balance Calculation

Demand letters must contain correct balances.

Configuration required:

Interest tariff reference
Interest calculation method
Balance date

6. Debt Threshold Rules

You normally do not issue legal notices for very small amounts.

Typical configuration:

Minimum balance for legal action
Minimum balance for summons

Example:

Final demand minimum: R500
Legal action minimum: R2 000

7. Approval Workflow

Debt packs normally require approval before legal instruction.

Configuration required:

Revenue Supervisor approval
Revenue Manager approval
CFO approval (optional)

System must log approvals.

8. Attorney Configuration

Legal notices may be issued by different attorneys.

Configuration required:

Attorney name
Firm name
Address
Email
Contact number
Reference format

9. Section 129 Document Numbering

Every legal notice must have a unique reference.

Configuration required:

Prefix
Sequence number
Financial year reset

Example:

LOD-2025-000124

10. Communication Templates

SMS / Email templates must be configurable.

Example SMS template:

Account {account_number} is overdue with a balance of {balance}.
Please settle within {days} days to avoid legal action.
